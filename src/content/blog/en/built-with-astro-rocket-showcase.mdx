The best thing that can land in a theme author's inbox isn't a star count or a download graph. It's an email that starts with *"I've deployed my latest venture…"*.

That email arrived last week, and it's the reason Astro Rocket now has a **[showcase](/showcase)** — a growing gallery of real sites built with the theme and running in production. This post introduces it, celebrates the site that started it, and explains how yours can be next.

## Why a showcase

A theme's demo can show you every component, every colour theme, and every Lighthouse score — but it can't answer the question that actually matters when you're choosing one: *what does this look like when someone builds something real with it?*

A showcase answers that. Real projects, real content, real deployments on real infrastructure — each one a different answer to "what can I do with this theme?" than the demo could ever give. And for me as the author, every entry is feedback from the place feedback matters most: production.

So from today, the showcase lives at **[astrorocket.dev/showcase](/showcase)**, with a companion list in the [README](https://github.com/hansmartensdev/Astro-Rocket#showcase).

## The founding entry: LinkPress

The site that started it all is **[LinkPress](https://linkpress.app/)** by **Mithun A. Sridharan** — his latest venture, built with Astro Rocket and deployed on Cloudflare. LinkPress is a content-syndication service with a sharp one-line pitch: *publish once, get discovered everywhere*. It gets content from businesses, startups, marketers, and non-profits in front of Google, AI assistants, and the LLMs their customers already use to find answers — with 900+ articles published and 24-hour Google indexing.

Mithun wrote to tell me about the launch and generously offered the site as a production example of the theme. That email is exactly the kind of moment that makes sharing a theme with the community worth it — and it seemed only right that LinkPress should be the showcase's first entry.

## Get your site listed

If you've built something with Astro Rocket, I'd love to feature it. The bar is deliberately low:

1. **Your site is live** and recognizably built with the theme.
2. **You open a [showcase submission](https://github.com/hansmartensdev/Astro-Rocket/issues/new?template=showcase_submission.yml)** on GitHub — the form asks for your live URL, a line or two about the project, and how you'd like to be credited.

That's it. The form also has two optional checkboxes — one allowing a screenshot on the showcase card, one allowing a short quote as a testimonial — tick whichever you're comfortable with.

Every submission gets a card on [the showcase](/showcase) with your name, your link, and your project. First come, first listed.
